# Finch Teardown — Collections & Customization

Area scope: Micropets, clothing/outfits, pet (birb) colors, furniture & house decoration, the wardrobe/saved-looks system, and every acquisition channel (shops, quests, seasonal events, journeys/travel, gifts, referrals, Guardian milestones).

Research date: 2026-06-11. Primary sources: official help center (help.finchcare.com), the community Finch Fandom wiki (finch.fandom.com — the most numerically precise source; all values below cross-checked there), app store listings, r/finch.

Currency note: nearly everything in this area is bought with **Rainbow Stones** (RS), the app's only currency. There are NO paid microtransactions for items — Finch monetizes only via the Finch Plus subscription ($9.99/mo or $69.99/yr standard pricing; loyalty discounts at 25/50/75/100 adventure-days reduce the yearly price 19%→$78.99 (from a higher anchor), 32%→$65.99, 39%→$58.99, 45%→$52.99) ([help.finchcare.com/.../Finch-Plus-Pricing](https://help.finchcare.com/hc/en-us/articles/38755205001869-Finch-Plus-Pricing), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finch_Plus](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finch_Plus)).

Key design rule repeated by the devs: **there are no member-exclusive items.** Finch Plus only gives more slots, earlier/guaranteed access, color choice, and discounts ([finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finch_Plus](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finch_Plus)).

## 1. Micropets

Sources: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Micropets](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Micropets), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Micropet_Playland](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Micropet_Playland), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Professor_Oat's_Lab](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Professor_Oat%27s_Lab), official help: [Using the Micropet Lab](https://help.finchcare.com/hc/en-us/articles/37780505907469-Using-the-Micropet-Lab).

### 1.1 What they are
- Small companion pets owned by the player's birb. **62 total as of June 2026** (one new pet added per monthly seasonal event).
- Each micropet has unique walk/stand/sleep animations and a unique sound ("voice") played on tap.
- Each has: name (editable), pronouns (editable), growth stage (baby/adult), adventure count, a **Nature** (random personality label; known values include Cheerful, Gentle, Quiet, Loyal, Playful, Brave — purely cosmetic, unrelated to color), and a flavor description.
- **3 color variants per pet** (original + 2 alternates); a handful of recent pets are single-color (Raven the Cat: black only; Cheshire Cat: purple only; Toto the Dog: brown only; Krypto: white only).
- Growth: hatches as **baby**; becomes **adult after accompanying the birb on 15 adventures**. A per-pet settings toggle ("growable to adult") lets it stay a "forever baby"; the toggle disappears once adult. Adults are larger with minor art changes.
- One micropet can be **equipped** to accompany the birb on its daily adventure (this is how adventure count accrues).
- Pets can be **released** from the settings screen.

### 1.2 Acquisition channels (5)
1. **Professor Oat's Lab egg hatching (primary loop):**
   - Lab is reached via Bag tab (5th bottom tab) → Micropets → "Lab" icon (Professor Oat's head, top-right of Playland). If the player has no egg, Professor Oat also proactively pops up to offer one.
   - The egg must be **linked to one repeating goal** of the player's choice. Completing that goal **7 times hatches the egg** into a **random** micropet (random species AND random one of its 3 color variants).
   - Only one goal linked at a time; **unlinking/relinking resets progress to 0/7**. Progress popup appears on each goal completion; progress is also visible from the egg tile in the Playland grid or via Lab → "Check the egg's progress."
   - Hatch animation: notification popup → egg cracks → reveal card introducing the new pet.
   - Immediately eligible for a new egg after hatching (visit the Lab to take one right away).
   - The pool mixes "common" pets and all past seasonal pets. **Duplicates are possible and unlimited** (devs have said they're considering tuning the duplicate algorithm).
2. **Seasonal events:** each monthly event awards its themed micropet on **reward day 25 (free)** or **day 20 (Finch Plus)** of the event track (see §6.1). After the event ends, that pet joins the random egg pool.
3. **Referral milestone:** inviting **3 new users** who join awards **Cookie the Cow** (not exclusive; also hatchable randomly). See §7.
4. **Being referred:** a new user who joins via a friend's invite link receives a **chosen micropet egg** (the inviter picks which pet to send; choices as of Feb 2025: Oatmeal the Llama, Talon the Gryphon, Pinecone the Hedgie, Boopty the Spoopty, Blizzard the Wizard, Aurora the Arctic Fox). One-time only.
5. **Co-op egg hatching with friends** (newer feature; hatch eggs together — wiki notes it is new/buggy).

There is no shop that sells micropets; they are never purchasable with Rainbow Stones.

### 1.3 "Rarity"
There is no formal in-game rarity stat. Effective tiers: 16 "common" pets (released straight into the egg pool in two batches of 8), 1 referral pet, 1 invite-gift pet, and ~44 seasonal pets (rarer in the egg pool; black-chest/event-limited at release). Plushies (a clothing held-item, §3) are the "legendary"-priced collectibles, not micropets.

### 1.5 Micropet Playland UI (collection screen)
- Bag tab → Micropets opens the **Playland**: a grassy scene showing the birb plus up to **10 owned micropets** roaming with individual movement (walk/hop/fly, some hide behind bushes). Tapping a pet plays its sound.
- Below the scene: a **grid of green tiles**, one per owned micropet, with the currently-incubating **egg (with progress bar) as the first tile**. Toggleable to a **list view** showing name, baby/adult state, and adventure count.
- Tapping a pet opens its detail card: name, stage, pronouns, adventures count, nature, description; **Equip** button (take on today's adventure); gear icon → settings (rename, pronouns, growable toggle, release).
- **Micropedia** (paw + magnifying-glass icon): collection index of all species. For each species you see which of the 3 color variants you own and duplicate counts; un-owned entries show as "?" silhouettes. Top-right "Lab" button → Professor Oat.

## 2. Pet ("birb") colors — The Color Studio

Sources: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Colors](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Colors), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/The_Color_Studio](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/The_Color_Studio), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Stages_of_Growth](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Stages_of_Growth).

### 2.1 Dye system
- Colors are **dye bottles**, each a single color for a single body part. Bought in the **Color Studio** shop (NPC: **Da Finci**) with Rainbow Stones. **235 dyes total** currently:

| Body part | # dyes | Price per dye | Unlocks at growth stage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Body | 36 | 900 RS | Toddler (after 7 adventures) |
| Beak | 24 | 300 RS | Toddler |
| Headpatch | 38 | 500 RS | Child (after 22 adventures) |
| Wings | 39 | 500 RS | Child |
| Cheeks | 32 | 300 RS | Teen (after 42 adventures) |
| Feet | 34 | 300 RS | Teen |
| Tummy/Belly | 32 | 500 RS | Adult (after 67 adventures) |

- **Growth-gated customization:** new account picks an egg color (Blue, Orange, Pink, Green, Purple, or Gray) = baby body color. Baby: yellow beak, gray feet, no customization. At each stage-up the game grants a free random color for the newly unlocked part(s) (toddler: pick 1 of 3 random beak colors; child: random headpatch+wings; teen: random cheeks+feet; adult: random tummy) and the corresponding dye shelf unlocks in the Studio.
- Dye color palettes per part are fixed lists (e.g., body: Gray, Tan, Blue, Purple, Pink, Green, Beige, Light Gray, Peach, Teal, Red, Yellow, Aqua, Orange, Brown, Baby Pink, Baby Blue, Black, Ivory, Light Orange, Lavender, 5 Neons, Dark Orange, Sage Green, Slate Blue, Rose Pink, Light Red, Olive, White, Lilac, Yellow Green, Dark Brown). Cheek dyes render slightly translucent.

### 2.2 Color Studio shop mechanics
- Daily rotation of **12 dye slots**; free users can buy from **6**, Finch Plus from all **12**, plus a daily **50%-off dye** banner slot (Plus only).
- No member-exclusive dyes — Plus only sees more slots.
- Auto-refresh every 24h (aligned to the birb's day cycle). Manual refresh: 1st free daily, then **10, 35, 60, 85, 110, 135, 160 RS** (cumulative 595 RS/day max).
- **Catalog** (try on any dye, can't buy from catalog) and **Sell-back at 50% of purchase price**; dyes currently worn can't be sold. **Dyes cannot be gifted** (unlike clothing/furniture).
- Free users can save a limited number of color combos; **Plus = unlimited saved color combinations** ([Finch_Plus](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finch_Plus)).

## 3. Clothing / outfit system — Mr. Prickles' Shop

Sources: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Clothing](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Clothing), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Prickles'_Shop](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Prickles%27_Shop), [Shops in Finch (official)](https://help.finchcare.com/hc/en-us/articles/37935977276813-Shops-in-Finch-Outfits-Travel-and-More), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Shops](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Shops).

### 3.1 Categories & pricing
- **10 clothing slots/categories**: Tops, Bottoms, Full Body, Head, Face, Neck, Feet, Held Item, Wings (capes/backpacks/wings), plus Guardian-only **Sparkle Effects** (cosmetic particle aura; not purchasable — see §7.3).
- Standard price points: **300 / 500 / 900 RS** (900 is the dominant tier: of ~900 cataloged items, ~283 at 900, ~141 at 500, ~49 at 300 on the wiki's tables). Outliers: **Legendary Plushies** held items — birb plushie **20,000 RS**, NPC plushies (Mr. Prickles, Sassafras, Da Finci, Robin, Professor Oat) **15,000 RS** each, 8–12 color variants ([Legendary_Plushie_Collection](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Legendary_Plushie_Collection)).
- Most items come in ~9–12 colors (standard palette: Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple, Pink, Black, White/Gray, Brown; some themed palettes, e.g., Sprinkles Top in ice-cream-flavor colors; Pride Flag Tee in 15+ flag colorways at 500 RS, Accessibility variants 300 RS).
- Content volume: **44 seasonal events** (Oct 2022–Jun 2026) each shipped ~6–12 clothing items + furniture, **16 pre-event clothing/furniture Collections** (Aug 2021–Sep 2022: Island, Halloween, Daily, Winter Holiday, PJ, Snow, Valentine's Day, Elegant & Royal, Animal Onesie, Cottage & Gardening, Hippie, Pride, Fruity, Sporty, Preppy + Accessibility), plus per-location exclusives (§5) ([Collections](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Collections)).

### 3.2 Equipping (the Bag)
- Bag tab → **Clothing**: owned items grouped by category; **tap to equip/unequip** instantly on the live birb.
- **Saved outfits ("favorites"):** free users can save **2 outfit combos**; **Finch Plus = unlimited** saved outfit, room, and color combos ([Finch_Plus](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finch_Plus)).

### 3.3 Mr. Prickles' Shop layout & mechanics (clothing shop)
Shop tab (3rd bottom tab) → choose one of 4 shops (Outfit / Furniture / Color / Travel); switchable via bottom bar afterwards. RS balance top-left; **Catalog** + **Sell** icons top-right; shopkeeper greeting window; refresh row; then item slots; then Everyday Collection.

- **Daily gift:** opening the Outfit shop grants **65–80 free Rainbow Stones** from Mr. Prickles once per day ([official help](https://help.finchcare.com/hc/en-us/articles/37935977276813-Shops-in-Finch-Outfits-Travel-and-More)).
- **Rotating "rare item" slots — 12 total**: free users may buy from the **first 6**; Plus unlocks all **12** + a **13th daily 50%-off** sale slot (banner at top; free users see a join-Plus ad instead). Items appear in a **fixed random color** (color is part of the listing name and cannot be changed). Any item can land in any slot — no Plus-only items.
- **Slot 1 = location item slot** (map-pin icon, distinct background): rotates through the 4 clothing exclusives of the birb's current location (e.g., Finchie Forest offers Acorn Hat / Mushroom Cap / Squirrel Costume / Hood).
- **Weighted rotation:** the month after a seasonal event, that event's items appear very frequently (and free players can then get them in all 10 colors, vs. the event's 4). Anniversary months boost the odds of same-month items from prior years. **October and December (Halloween/holiday) items are hard-exclusive** to their month + the 2 preceding weeks; never appear otherwise.
- **Plushies** occasionally appear in rotation (random color), priced 15k/20k RS as long-term sinks.
- **Everyday Collection** (below the slots): a permanent catalog of basics — always purchasable, **player picks any color** via a swatch scroller on the item sheet. Includes the full Pride, Accessibility, PJ, Daily, and Snow collections plus assorted staples (Everyday Cap 500, Bear Hug Tee 300, Comfy Pants 300, bikini/trunks 300–500, Classic Polo/Pants/Skirt 900, Oval Glasses 500, Classic Necktie 500, Teddy Bear 500, Ice Pop 300, etc.) ([Everyday_Collection](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Everyday_Collection)).
- **Catalog / try-on:** magnifying-glass icon opens the full game catalog by category incl. every location exclusive; player can preview anything on their birb in any color; reference prices shown; **no purchasing** from the catalog.
- **Sell:** stone-bag icon; sell any owned clothing back for **50% of purchase price** (500→250). Sold items are removed from inventory and from saved outfits.
- **Refresh:** auto every 24h (countdown clock shown). Manual: first free daily, then 10/35/60/85/110/135/160 RS escalating (cap; total daily spend 595 RS). New roll replaces all 12 slots.
- **Duplicates:** you can own one of each item+color combo. Owned color combos are marked "SOLD" and stop appearing in rotation until sold/gifted away.
- **Gifting from the shop:** when buying, choose "Send Gift" to a Tree Town friend for **+200 RS fee**; friend list shows "Owned" (they have it) or "Gifted" (already gifted them today — 1 gift per friend per day). Same item can be gifted to multiple friends while it's in stock; if you want one for yourself + gifts, gift first, then buy (owning it disables the shop gift button). Gifting from inventory is also possible.

## 4. Furniture & house decoration — Finkea Furnishings + the Birbhouse

Sources: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Furniture](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Furniture), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finkea_Furnishings](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Finkea_Furnishings), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Birbhouse](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Birbhouse), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Floors](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Floors).

### 4.1 The Birbhouse (single room, fixed-slot decoration)
- Every player has **one room** ("birbhouse"). There is no free-form placement and no multiple rooms — decoration is **slot-based**: each category has exactly one fixed position in the room. Double-tap the home screen to toggle the birb between current travel location and its house. Birb sleeps in the bed during user-set sleep hours, sits/waves on the rug otherwise; petting (finger swipe) produces hearts and builds Friendship.
- **14 decoration slots** in fixed positions:
  - **Furniture (10):** Bed (left side; birb sleeps here), Rug (room center floor), Doormat (under door), Door Item Left ("plant" slot) & Door Item Right ("skateboard" slot), Wall Item (above bed, left of window), Clock (above door, right of window), Lamp (ceiling center, hanging), Dresser (under window), Dresser Item (on the dresser).
  - **Interior (4):** Floor, Wall(paper), Door (right side), Window (wall center).
- **Changing furnishings:** Bag tab → Furniture → items grouped by slot type → tap to place (instant swap).
- **Saved rooms ("favorites"):** heart icon to favorite a full room layout; **free = 2 saved rooms, Plus = unlimited**; tap a favorite to re-apply the whole look instantly.

### 4.2 Finkea Furnishings shop (NPC: Robin)
Mechanics mirror Mr. Prickles' exactly:
- **12 rotating rare slots** (6 free / 12 Plus) + Plus-only daily **50%-off** banner item; fixed random colors; 24h auto-refresh; manual refresh free then 10/35/60/85/110/135/160 RS; catalog try-in-room (no purchase); sell-back at 50% (300→150, 500→250, 900→450); duplicate color lockout; 200 RS gift fee to friends.
- **Location slot:** each travel location has **4 exclusive furniture items** (max 4 colors each) sold only while the birb is there (lighter background + globe/map marker).
- **Seasonal slots:** October/December event furniture appears only in those months (+2 weeks lead-in).
- **Everyday Collection (furniture):** permanent basics in choice of color — includes the Simple, Treehouse, Funky, and Contemporary collections (the four pre-event furniture collections, Aug–Sep 2022), the Pride Wall Shelf, **plain wallpaper in 12 colors** (last item), and **Floors** (second-to-last item).
- **Floors:** ~**50 floor colorways in 5 styles** (Traditional, Dark, Light, Vibrant, Vintage), **500 RS each**, ~12 colors per style, always available.
- Pricing distribution on the wiki's full tables: 300 / 500 / 900 RS tiers (900 most common; ~361 items at 900, ~267 at 500, ~12 at 300).

## 5. Travel/journeys as an acquisition channel (Travel with Sass)

Sources: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Travel_with_Sass](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Travel_with_Sass), [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Locations](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Locations).

- Travel Agency shop (NPC: Sassafras) unlocks at **Child stage (22 full-energy days)**. First flight free; thereafter **300 RS per one-way flight** (return to Finchie Forest: **200 RS**). Flight consumes the day's adventure (requires full energy) and yields a Discovery on landing.
- **27 permanent locations + 3 seasonal story locations** (as of May 2026). Daily random destination offers: **free = 3 choices, Plus = 9**; the Travel shop **cannot be refreshed**.
- Each location's detail popup shows completion %, special discoveries found, and previews its **4 exclusive clothing items + 4 exclusive furniture items** — these only stock in Mr. Prickles'/Finkea while you're at that location (e.g., Tokyo Tee 900 RS, Norway Sweater 900 RS, Capybara Tee 500 RS, Icelandic Puffin Tee 500 RS). This makes travel a core collection driver.
- Logbook (notes menu) tracks visited locations, % completion, per-location discoveries; 100% completion colorizes the location's picture.

## 6. Events, quests, and other earn loops feeding collections

### 6.1 Seasonal events (monthly battle-pass-lite)
Source: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Seasonal_Events](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Seasonal_Events).
- One per month since Oct 2022 (**44 events** as of Jun 2026); lives at the top of the Quests tab as a day-by-day reward calendar. Earn 1 reward per day by reaching **full energy** that day.
- **Free column:** mystery chests or RS. **Orange chest** = random event clothing item; **purple chest** = random event furniture item; **black chest** = 88% random event item / 12% Rainbow Stones. Item drops let the user pick from **4 predetermined colors**. Random → duplicates possible, completion not guaranteed. **Day 25 = event micropet.**
- **Plus column (additional, on top of free column):** first ~25 rewards are **specific items** (deterministic — guarantees the full set), color chosen from **10 colors**; **day 20 = micropet**; after all items, further days give chests (still 10-color choice).
- Events can be disabled (Settings → Account Preferences → Seasonal Events).
- Post-event: 2-week claim window for earned-but-unclaimed rewards; items then enter shop rotation (heavily for one month, anniversary-boosted after; Oct/Dec items month-locked). Missed micropets remain obtainable via random eggs.

### 6.2 Quests (Rainbow Stone faucets)
Source: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Quests](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Quests).
- **Daily quests: 25 RS each** (e.g., "change one interior item," "change your birb's outfit," "send Good Vibes," "complete a goal," breathing/affirmation/journaling tasks) — note several dailies directly incentivize using the customization systems.
- **Weekly milestones:** per self-care area, complete ≥1 goal on 2/4/6 days → **20/50/100 RS**.
- **Special quests: 100 RS per tier**, with collection-themed ladders: "Collect N pieces of clothing" and "Obtain N decorations" (N = 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 75, 100, 150, 200, 300, 400, 500, 750, 1000, 1200, 1500, 2000, 2500, 3000, 4000, 5000, 10000), "Meet N micropets" (1, 3, 5, 8, 11, 15, 20, 40, 60, 100, 125, 150), "Visit N locations" (2, 3, 5, 7 … 27), birb growth stages, friendship levels.
- Other RS sources: goal completions (boosted by "Goal of the Day"), full-energy adventures, Good Vibes (first/day = 2 RS + 3 energy), reflections, selling items, daily 65–80 RS shop gift ([Rainbow_Stones](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Rainbow_Stones)).

### 6.3 Friend gifting (Tree Town)
Source: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Tree_Towns](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Tree_Towns), [Mr._Prickles'_Shop](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._Prickles%27_Shop).
- Clothing & furniture (not dyes) can be gifted from shop stock (+**200 RS** fee) or from inventory; **1 gift per friend per day**; "Owned"/"Gifted" states enforced in the picker.

## 7. Referrals, Guardians & misc. exclusive acquisition

### 7.1 Referral ladder (Invite)
Source: [finch.fandom.com/wiki/Invite](https://finch.fandom.com/wiki/Invite). Rewards per accepted **new-user** invite (max 3, subject to change):
1. 1st invite: Rainbow Stones + an **animal hood** (clothing)
2. 2nd invite: a **plushie** held item (Grey Finch Plushie)
3. 3rd invite: micropet **Cookie the Cow**
- The invited friend picks up the inviter's chosen **micropet egg** on joining (§1.2).

## 8. Rebuild notes (mechanics checklist for 1:1 Telegram Mini App)

1. **Single currency (RS)** with faucets: dailies 25, weeklies 20/50/100, specials 100/tier, daily shop gift 65–80, goal/energy/vibes trickle; sinks: items (300/500/900), dyes (300/500/900), flights (200/300), refresh ladder (10→160), gift fee (200), streak repair, plushies (15k/20k).

Travel shop = no refresh, 3/9 choices.
3. **Random-color scarcity** is the core collection retention trick: wanted item+color combos only via random rotation; Everyday items are choice-color; event months & anniversaries bias the RNG; Oct/Dec hard exclusivity windows.
4. **Egg = habit anchor**: 7 goal-completions per hatch, single linked goal, reset on relink, infinite re-issue, random species+variant w/ duplicates.
5. **Stage-gated cosmetic unlocks** tie customization depth to long-term engagement (toddler 7 / child 22 / teen 42 / adult 67 adventures).
6. **Premium = convenience, never exclusivity**: more slots, deterministic event items + 10-color choice, micropet on day 20 vs 25, unlimited saved outfits/rooms/colors (free: 2/2/limited), daily discount, 9 travel choices. Keep a "no member-only items" guarantee for community goodwill; sparkle effects are donor-recognition, not paywall.
